Fashion Weeks around the World

Paris Fashion Week

Paris Fashion Week is a series of designer presentations held semiannually in Paris, France with spring/summer and autumn/winter events held each year. Dates are determined by the French Fashion Federation. Paris Fashion Week is held at venues throughout the city.

New York Fashion Week

New York Fashion Week held in February and September of each year, is a semi-annual series of events in Manhattan typically spanning 7â€“9 days when international fashion collections are shown to buyers, the press, and the general public.

London Fashion Week

London Fashion Week is a clothing trade show that takes place in London twice a year, in February and September. Showcasing over 250 designers to a global audience of influential media and retailers, it is one of the 'Big Four' fashion weeks, along with the New York, Milan and Paris.
